Our Story

A Sanctuary of Taste & Refinement

Royal Spice was born from a singular obsession: to transform dining into theatre. Every dish is composed like a sonata — layered, intentional, unforgettable — drawing from heritage spice routes and modern gastronomy alike.

Set within a restored heritage hall of warm brass and obsidian marble, our room is designed to make every guest feel like royalty. The lighting is low, the service is silent, and the flavours are loud.

The Artisan

The Mind Behind The Magic

"A plate should tell a story the moment it touches the table."

Trained across Paris, Tokyo and the royal kitchens of Rajasthan, Chef Arjun fuses classical European technique with the soul of Indian spice. His tasting menu changes with the seasons, sourced from the finest local farms and rare global imports.

Twice awarded Michelin stars, his philosophy is simple — restraint, precision, and an unrelenting pursuit of the perfect bite.
